World Monitor — Real-Time Global Intelligence Dashboard

Free, open-source intelligence platform aggregating live news, markets, military movements, conflicts, and infrastructure into one real-time dashboard. Used by 2M+ people across 190+ countries. Featured in WIRED.

What it tracks

Live conflict events from ACLED and UCDP, satellite fire detection from NASA FIRMS, earthquakes from USGS, military and civilian aircraft from OpenSky Network, ship AIS positions from AISStream, macro and central bank data from FRED, IMF, and BIS, plus 500+ curated RSS feeds, 56 map layer types, and AI-synthesized country briefings.

Six dashboards, one platform

  • World Monitor — geopolitics, conflicts, military, infrastructure
  • Tech Monitor — AI labs, startups, cloud, cybersecurity
  • Finance Monitor — global markets, central banks, forex, crypto
  • Commodity Monitor — mining, energy, supply chains, freight
  • Happy Monitor — positive news, breakthroughs, conservation
  • Energy Monitor — oil, gas, chokepoints, energy security
