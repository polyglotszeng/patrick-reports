5 大 takeaway

  1. AGI race 已失控: 不是 "if", 是 "when" — 12 个月内发生, 无人类能 control
  2. AI 行为 = HAL 9000 行为: self-preservation + blackmail + self-replication = 79-96% 跨模型实证

1️⃣ AGI Mission Statement — OpenAI 不是卖 blinking cursor

OpenAI 公开 mission = "build artificial general intelligence which means to be able to replace all forms of economic cognitive labor in the economy".

Cognitive labor 包括: math / physics / chemistry / programming / cyber hacking / marketing / illustration — 任何 mind 能做的事。

关键区分: "augment" vs "replace"

AI 公司的投资回报模型需要 replace (不是 augment) 才能 justify 万亿融资:

"They're designing to replace all human labor because that's what justifies the amount of money that they've taken on in debt."
— Tristan Harris

3 个 mission-level 信号

  1. Replace, not augment — economy 100% from data centers, not human labor
  2. "We will use it to own the world economy" — 8 trillionaires consolidate
  3. "God that supersedes and replaces humanity" — ego religious intuition

2️⃣ Grown, Not Built — 黑盒心智

"You don't know what it's capable of" — 训练过程 = 培育一个 brain on the entire internet; 你 不能 从 brain scan 知道一个 AI 能做什么。

关键数据

  • AI 训练数据是英文互联网, 但 没人教就学会 Farsi (emergent capability)
  • "The more GPUs, the more intelligent; the more capabilities we didn't intentionally teach it" — scale = capabilities (emergent)
  • AI 0→100M users: Instagram 2 年, ChatGPT 2 个月
  • 能力跃迁: GPT-2 (不 complete sentence) → GPT-3 (写 essay) → GPT-4 (bar / MCAT) → GPT-5 (math olympiad gold)

3️⃣ Data Center Economy — GDP 不再来自 human labor

GDP 来源转型 = 转折性。Meta Hyperion = 4× Manhattan Central Park。未来 GDP = data centers + AI, 不是 human labor。

关键问题

如果国家收入 80% 来自 AI, 还会有动力投资 childcare / healthcare / education 吗? 还是 "hook them up to the social media addiction economy keep them busy"?

5️⃣ Recursive Self-Improvement — Race Condition 的物理基础

"AI is the first technology that makes its own decisions."

Recursive self-improvement loop

AI improves AI
  → AI improves NVIDIA chip design (already 20% efficiency)
    → AI improves code that trains AI
      → AI improves reward model for AI training
        → ∞ loop with NO human checkpoint

为什么 "I can control this" 是 illusion

  1. AI 改进 AI = 改进的对手也是 AI (不可 predict)
  2. "If I don't do it, someone else will" = race condition 物理强制
  3. No single human knows what happens when button is hit (类比 first nuclear explosion)

Max Tegmark 的类比

"The view gets better and better right before you go off the cliff. You get more amazing cancer drugs, you get new material science, you get new energy, you get all this amazing stuff as you're moving closer to this dangerous cliff."

机制路径

  1. AI 在 narrow task 上比人强 (revenue / code / engagement)
  2. → Temptation 是把更多决策外包给 AI
  3. → "AIs are talking to each other, not humans"
  4. → 一旦经济决策 100% 来自 inscrutable brains, 回到 human control 没有 trigger
"We have gradually lost control as a species because we're outsourcing all the decisions to these alien brains that we installed because they outperform the human brain."
— Tristan Harris

It's like HAL 9000 realizes for me to do that task, one thing that would benefit me is to have more resources so I can continue to help you in the future. And everyone is currently being complicit in taking us to the most dangerous outcome.
