🔬 Distillation — 中国 AI 的 "cheat sheet"

Sacks 完整描述

"Picture a farm like that. Tens of thousands of phones, iPads, and computers that are asking the cloud API through masked accounts, very specific questions, and then these what's called reasoning traces are being harvested because if you're on the API, you know, you get to see every token. And those reasoning traces are then fed back into the model during the reinforcement learning process and probably during the pre-training process. And that is a way that you can get really really close to the frontier at a fraction of the cost."

类比

"It would be like asking Google every question you could ever imagine, every search imaginable, getting all the results and then putting your own search engine together to make it very simple."

关键时差

  • 9 个月 = 中国落后 frontier 模型的 baseline (Sacks 经验值, ±3 个月)
  • 但 distillation 缩小 gap + 在 breakthrough 方向上 "deploy more resources against that particular problem and catch up faster"
  • Z.AI 创始人给的 hard deadline = Q1 2027 = 约 6 个月内 达 fable-level

监管两难

Sacks: "If you just clamp down in a way that doesn't even allow these models to be used, the Chinese are going to have these capabilities imminently anyway... we have to basically get these tools in the hands of our cyber security industry. They're the force multiplier."

结论: 不可能通过 clampdown 拖慢中国 — 唯一能做的是"smart" cyber 防御 + 让 white hat 找漏洞 + 快速升级 cycle。

📦 Tesla Megapod — Modular data center trademark

Megapod trademark (6/18/2026 filing)

"Modular data center hardware for artificial intelligence computing comprised of network of computer servers, computer hardware for artificial intelligence processing, computer network hardware, electric power distribution units and cooling systems sold as a unit, self-contained modular computing hardware systems for artificial intelligent workloads..."

现场解读

"Essentially what people are saying is this is going to be a giant battery pack with GPUs at the supercharger stations which have already been approved. That's the back channel."

Chamath 数据中心投资者视角

"If you want to turn on compute terrestrially: land (relatively straightforward), zoning (less straightforward), power (very difficult)... The basis of design restrictions are being relaxed... we've moved to an architectural model from Google and Nvidia that has said look we have to liquid cool these racks. These are very complicated big girth supercomputer racks. And now we're also going back and saying maybe some of these older stuff that's a little bit less proficient... we can air cool them."

Chamath 给 Patrick 算账

"There are companies like Dell makes these racks, companies like Vertive makes these modules, and I think if Tesla can make them available, folks like us for my data center project, we would be enormous buyers of these things because we would just literally prefab them in a warehouse... Get the chips, prefab them, truck them to the place, crane them in, turn it on, off to the races you go. You have like a 90-day build cycle which is unheard of."

限制条件

  • 某些 workload 需要 liquid cooling, supercharger station 难满足
  • Security: 数据中心架构需要 man traps, fingerprint access, 隔离
  • 贡献给 distributed training pool 需要 SLA + liquidated damages

🔀 Inference Disaggregation — 下一个 AI infra 拐点

核心概念

Inference = 模型回答用户问题的过程, 可以拆成 2 步:

阶段本质硬件需求类比
Prefill理解问题 + 整理答案框架Memory capacity (越大越好)"记住的词越多越好"
Decode生成下一个 tokenMemory bandwidth (越快越好)"说得越快越好"

拆分后解锁的机会

"These two types of inference are increasingly being disaggregated... you can lift H100s, A100s out of some old data center, put them in one of these mega mega pods, you know, a rack in a shipping container, put a Groq or Cerebras in front of it, and you can get a very competitive solution."

关键论点: 老 GPU 重新可用

"We're going to be using GPUs for seven years, 10 years, 12 years, and that's great because it lowers the cost to finance them, which makes this AI revolution more financable."

Decode accelerator 选项

  • Groq (Chamath 投资人, Nvidia 收购)
  • Cerebras (CS-3 wafer-scale)
  • 都说能跑超 decode 的 workload, 但实际只 decode 优化

Distributed inference 失败模式

"If these things are far away from each other, meaning not right next to each other, they're so less efficient. Like the efficiency drops dramatically. You want these things to be right next to each other physically. You get like multiple orders of mag at least an order of magnitude type efficiency maybe plus-plus."

"You could have two facilities passing the jobs from one GPU to the other is super important and if it's on a peer-to-peer network it's going to have lag. Even if it's on your own fiber connected like 2 kilometers away you're screwed."

→ 分布式 inference ≠ 分布式 training。Latency 敏感 vs 带宽敏感, 不同。

Chamath Power Wall GPU 集成 (Elon)

"Every Power Wall has our GPUs in it. Part of the offering is you can't buy a Power Wall without putting GPUs in it. And when you're not using it, we will pay you for your battery. And you put a Starlink on your roof. And now you've got a distributed system where you get a couple of these power walls with their own silicon in it and a Starlink. And now Elon's created an infinite number of home battery backup systems... He gets the exclusive for 20 years."

Chamath "pan-American" distributed inference 看好

"At the intersection of competing against China, having a vibrant American open source community, having a bunch of distributed models for purposes of free speech and otherwise that Travis mentioned earlier, I do think this idea of distributed inference has a real place in the American ecosystem. I don't exactly know where and how and how homeowners would get paid, but whoever figures that out as a pan-American idea, I think is really onto something."
Patrick 视角: Inference disaggregation + 老 GPU 重新利用 是 Patrick hybrid-llm-router 的核心论点。Chamath 的 Power Wall GPU 集成想法 = 终极 distributed inference = Patrick 5-tool stack 的"个人 LLM 节点"哲学跟 Elon 商业模型的同源对齐。

💰 IPO 市场 — $4T Backlog, Anthropic $3T, Cerebras 跌破发行价

Anthropic $3T 估值 (Gavin 一手观点)

"I think Anthropic is worth $3 trillion today and it's very important... They're going to end this year well over $100 billion [revenue]... very profitable at that scale because it'll be inference-dominated and people reporting they have 85% gross margins on inference... in terms of the market absorbing this, it's just shifting from private to public... in the scale of global capital markets, these seem like really big numbers. You're just moving from the private markets to the public markets which are even bigger."

SpaceX IPO 流动性解析

  • 过去 10 年每 6 个月员工/投资人可卖, 不同于传统 IPO lockup
  • 几乎一半员工在 IPO 时买入 (非卖出)
  • 大部分 owner 通过 SPV 持有, 不会立即 distributed
  • → "不一定是市场想象的 liquidity wall"
  • Elon 肯定不卖, "obviously"

Cerebras 跌破发行价的 4 教训

  1. 银行家教训: "if you're going public... tell your bankers price this in such a way that we're not going to break deal price in our first nine months as a public company. That's what I always advise everyone to do and I think it's important"
  2. PM "pylon" 效应: "if a stock breaks deal price, they sell it no matter what... if stock breaks deal price it can sometimes go to places you wouldn't think it would go"
  3. Shorts target 接近发行价的股票: 主动 break deal price → make quick 10-20%
  4. 叙事学习期: "it takes companies a while to learn how to tell their story and communicate to public markets. It's a very different audience than VCs"

Gavin 对 Cerebras 投资逻辑 (买盘逻辑)

"How many megawatts can they bring on? Because we know what they're going to monetize at. And that is the question."

实际 metric = 能多快带来 power, 不是市场份额。Cerebras OpenAI $25B 合约 (12/2025 签) → Taiwan Semi wafer 4 个月 → 制造 2 个月 → 找到电 1 个月 → 最早 Labor Day 2026 开始贡献。预测: 50 MW/月 ramp → 2027 年底 exit $9B 年化 cloud computing run rate。

$4T Backlog 怎么 absorb

  • Anthropic + SpaceX + Cerebras + OpenAI = $4T 新供应
  • "It's not hard for global capital markets to absorb 50 billion of an offering... 5% float is 150 billion. There's enough"

历史对比: Uber IPO (Chamath 亲历)

"We got pillaried in the press for not knowing what we were doing. We priced Uber at 14 billion. Six months later it was 42 billion."

Chamath 当时在 Fidelity, 跟同事做 Uber IPO 定价 $14B (Dutch auction: "$14B → $42B 6 月后 round"), 那时候 $14B private company 是 "groundbreaking"。10 年后 = $4T offerings 是 baseline。
