Core Thesis

Fable 5 is built for long-running autonomous tasks — hours or days without babysitting. The shift: stop treating AI as a tool, start treating it as an employee on payroll. Fable runs until the job is done, tests its own work, and keeps looping.


The three Fable principles from Enthropic's Cloud Code team (Thark):

  1. Give it context like a thought partner
  2. Give it goals + verification methods
  3. Be more ambitious — ask it to do things you've never tried

1/goal — The "Contractor That Never Leaves"

With /goal command, Fable loops indefinitely until the job is done. Demo: /goal create me a complete Minecraft game. Fable went out, found libraries, built the app — without stopping at 5pm like previous models.

"It used to be when you're using other models, the contractor would just leave at 5:00 p.m. regardless. But with /goal, he does not leave until the job is done." — Samin

Pattern: Describe the app + define what "working" means + walk away. Fable self-corrects and keeps going.

2Subscription Cancellation — The $1,500/month Saver

Samin saved $1,500/month by having Fable audit and cancel unnecessary subscriptions automatically.

Step 1: Connect Your Bank

Plus → Connectors → Manage → Era Context (free, connects any bank). Gives Fable read access to transactions.

Step 2: Audit + Visualize

Ask: "break down my finances for the last month" → Fable generates pie charts + tables of subscriptions.

Step 3: Auto-Cancel via Chrome

Claude for Chrome + Fable's computer vision → navigates each subscription website, finds cancel button, completes cancellation. Samin ran this on his Mac Mini overnight.

"It's doing a bunch of stuff... going through and keeping doing it for you rather than you having to do it."

3Marketing Agent — Hicksfield + Fable

Marketing = 10 jobs at once (strategy, copy, directing, production, QA). Fable has "eyes" (visual taste) + keeps improving prompts based on failures.

Demo: Claude Club Ads Campaign

Prompt: "Set a goal to make a full set of ads, images, and videos. Use a workflow to find and scrape things from school.com/cloud and create all image+video ads using Hicksfield MCP. Do them all in parallel. Put approved/rejected in folders."

Fable scraped the site, found scripts, generated 60-second motion graphic videos + image ads, auto-rejected bad ones (wrong glasses, spelling errors), organized into approved/rejected folders. Samin called the output "really usable."

4Stock Trading Research Agent — Firecrawl + Debating Agents

Fable spins up parallel sub-agents that each do deep research, then debate each other. Output: the answer that survives all challenges.

The Hypothesis Test Pattern

"I have a hunch Tesla bottoms at $360. Prove it or destroy it. Pull 2 years of real price data. Back-test the hypothesis. Check politician trades. Check hedge fund moves. Have agents challenge each other. I want the answer that survives all arguments, not the first answer."

Fable found Samin's hunch was wrong — buying the dips underperformed just holding Tesla. It told him honestly even though he was wrong.

6Sales Outreach Agent — Clay + Researcher Agents

Finding + researching + personalizing outreach for hundreds of prospects is the hardest part of selling services. Clay brings verified data (150+ providers), Fable handles the endurance work.

The Prompt

"Use Clay to find 10 YouTube creators in my niche (10-200k subs). For each, spin up a researcher agent, go through their recent videos, find their content, get their verified email. Draft a 3-sentence message referencing one of their top videos, naming a gap, offering one specific fix. Checker agent verifies before sending."

"Clay can hand us 100 perfect contacts, but someone still has to research them and write each message — that's hundreds of tasks back and forth. That's exactly what Fable is good at."
