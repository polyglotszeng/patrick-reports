Method

Each 5° grid cell's population is built from a multi-country, area-weighted allocation: each country's polygon is intersected with every cell's 5° box, and the country's POP_EST is split across its contributing cells in proportion to (area_share × cos latitude). GDP per capita comes from the World Bank official 2023 series NY.GDP.MKTP.CD (current US$) for all reporting economies; countries absent from the WB dataset (e.g. North Korea, Cuba, Syria, Taiwan, Yemen, South Sudan, Kosovo, and disputed territories like Western Sahara, Northern Cyprus, Somaliland) fall back to Natural Earth's GDP_MD as a best-effort estimate. Per cell, GDP/capita is the pop-weighted average across contributing countries. Service value at a cell = overhead_count × pop_in_cell × gdp_per_capita, an economically-weighted coverage proxy.
