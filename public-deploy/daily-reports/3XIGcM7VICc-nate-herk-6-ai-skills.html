🎤 Section-by-Section

0:27-4:39 · Skill 1: The AI Person

起点低, 圈子里比你同事懂就行 — "becoming the AI person is relative"。路径: hobby → show 同事 ("周末用 Claude 清了一个 3 小时流程到 20 分钟") → 内部 "AI lead" 候选人。引用 IBM 2026 CEO study: "85% CEOs said all functional leaders must become technology experts" (注: 真实数字 85% 跟 "success depends on people" 不是同义)。

实操: 选 1 个主 AI tool + 1 个工作流 + 文档化 before/after。

4:39-7:38 · Skill 2: Taste & Judgment

AI 输出越好, 人越容易 "let your guard down" → 失去 review。例子: em dashes — 5 个 em dash 出来 = "AI 写的", 读者会怀疑。

"AI can generate the work. Taste is deciding what deserves your name." — Nate, 7:14

实操 3 步: ① Study best work in your field ② Save examples, ask "为什么这是好的" ③ Feedback loop: 每次纠正 AI, 写下来 "改了什么, 为什么", 更新 instructions。

7:38-10:06 · Skill 3: Context Engineer ⭐

"Prompts are how you ask, context is what your AI actually knows" (引 Karpathy 2025)。Nate 自己的 "AI OS" = meeting transcripts + YouTube videos + DMs + emails, 比自己 recall 还好。关键: 从 blank chat 转到 Claude Projects / Custom GPT。

"Without context, AI is just a smart intern who's guessing." — Nate, 9:44

关键 insight: "context about your brain" = 不可公开访问的 data = 你的护城河。

10:06-13:09 · Skill 4: Iteration Speed ⭐ 实战可借鉴

2 个 skill 配合: taste (skill 2) 知道 good 长啥样, iteration 尽快到那里。比喻: 教 kid 骑车 — 一次跑完 1 mile 不可能, 要 calibrate 一次又一次。

实操 4 步:

  1. Master keyboard shortcuts (减少 mouse)
  2. 用 voice input (推自己的 Glydo tool, 卖课信号 ⚠️)
  3. Rapid prototyping: 不要 plan perfect, 跑 ugly version 试错
  4. Definition of Done ⭐ — 1 metric = 1 done 条件 (e.g. "tickets resolved per day" / "qualified appointments set per week"), 达到了切 maintenance mode
"The second you remove yourself from the process, a system has to be pretty air-tight and pretty battle-tested." — Nate, 14:24

13:09-16:38 · Skill 5: Build Your Jarvis ⭐ 实战可借鉴

Iron Man 类比: Jarvis 一直在 background, 自己 fire, kick off task 不用 Tony 按按钮。关键区分:

Vending Machine vs Slot Machine:

  • 🥤 Vending machine = deterministic workflow, 不需要 AI (e.g. 每天 9:00 拉 Stripe revenue → Slack)
  • 🎰 Slot machine = AI agent, reasoning + 生成 (e.g. 读 customer email, draft tailored response)

决策 2 问: ① Do I need to be the trigger? Or system can fire itself? ② Does this step need AI? Or simple Python script / no-code workflow?

"Default to the simplest thing that gets the job done." — Nate, 16:23

反模式: "people who win in AI era aren't building fanciest agents with hundreds of tools, they're building systems that run quietly in the background"。

16:38-20:14 · Skill 6: Unemployment Insurance / Job Stacking

1 job 1 income 旧模式脆弱 → 1 day job + 2-3 AI side income streams。不要 5 个不同域: 选 1 North Star (passion) → 多 shape 包装。

Shape 例子: course / niche newsletter / micro SaaS / consulting — 都 same expertise。Building in public = 写小工具 + share 学习 + document wins/losses。

AI-search 时代 insight: 如果你不在 AI 能找到的地方 (blog / 视频 / 公网), 搜不到 = 存在不了。
