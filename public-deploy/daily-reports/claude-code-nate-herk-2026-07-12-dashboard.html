5 大主线

1. AI Native ≠ 用 AI 做每件事

AI Native 是"先默认用 AI,包括自动化自身流程",且只在约束 (constraints) 上投入工作,其他都是 busywork。"Constraint is the only place where work compounds. Everywhere else you're just busy."
