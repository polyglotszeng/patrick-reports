🦜 LLM 不是真推理 = "Stochastic Parrots"

Matt 1 年前立场（2026 仍 stand）

"The reasoning is not a kind of reasoning that is human reason. It's kind of sort of like a fake reasoning. The models can't reason."

为什么不是真推理？

  • Next token prediction = mimic human reasoning through text
  • 通过 human examples replicate
  • "We don't generally reason out in tokens or written aspect. We reason through our brains."

关键承认

"The reasoning that we're seeing with LLMs is not the same degree of reasoning at human level, but the reasoning that we do have in LLMs is no less useful."

关键警告

"The personification of LLMs is a little bit harmful."

"These are systems. They're not people."

Stochastic Parrots 比喻（Bender 原始）

"A stochastic parrot can replicate the human phrase or even a sentence without actually understanding that."

"It's pattern matched data... sometimes it's just going to echo back our own thoughts to us."

🛡️ Safety by Design（Agentic 时代）

安全 4 层（同 web 架构）

层例子

Network传输安全
InterfaceAPI / user interface

Agent safety 特别挑战

"When you don't really have that much of control unlike when you're just working with one particular model."

Multi-agent safety 必须 built into

  • Framework（contain agent）
  • Harness（constrain behavior）
  • Protocol layer（enforce safety）
  • Framework itself（running the agent）

"Safety is often a last thought when working hard at innovation."

"Look at these considerations not just from security on the network but also downstream effects on society, on environment."
