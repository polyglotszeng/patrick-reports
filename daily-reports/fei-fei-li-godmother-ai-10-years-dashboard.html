教育目标（Fei-Fei 关键立场）

"The goal of education is not a tool. The goal of education is not closed book or open book exams. The goal of education is not standardized test scores. The goal of education is building humans so that every individual is a meaningful contributor of their community and society and lead to a meaningful life."

需要重思的 5 件事

  1. 教室（classroom）
  2. 考试（examinations）
  3. 标准化测试（standardized test）

👥 2 种 Worker

🏆 Top 1% Specialist

  • 极深 mastery
  • 顶级 copywriter / 设计师 / 工程师
  • AI 也替代不了
  • "I can't beat you at it"
  • 代表：MasterClass 老师 / World Labs 研究员

🌍 High-Agency Generalist

  • 多领域 breadth
  • 创业者 / PM / 跨学科
  • judgment + 创造力 + 工具驾驭
  • "如果大家说 good idea, probably not"
  • 代表：创始人 / 高 agency 员工

❌ 被 AI 替代 = "okay" 的人（中间 98%）

"If I'm an okay copywriter, anybody that now uses an LLM can do a decent job at that. But if you're the best in the world at it or if you're in the top 1%, I can't beat you at it."

Barbell Effect

"What I'm seeing is a bifurcation of those specialists and the people that can do lots of things well."

= 哑铃型分布（specialist 顶尖 + generalist 高 agency）

Evolution 时间尺度

⚠️ Discourse 极端化

现状（Fei-Fei 警告）

🌟 极端 A：godlike

  • "AI 会拯救世界"
  • "我们不再需要工作"
  • "躺着拿工资"

💀 极端 B：devil

  • "AI 替代所有工作"
  • "抢走人类 agency"
  • "毁灭一切"

缺什么：Nuanced Middle

"The most important discourse is missing. And that's the discourse of the nuanced middle. What is this tool? How can we use it for good? How should we avoid the pitfalls? And how do we go forward as a civilization with this civilizational tool?"

Fei-Fei 立场

"AI is a tool. It's a very powerful tool. But it's a tool that humans can wield to help make things better. But we also have to be very very vigilant about how to use the tool."

不 adapt 的代价（spreadsheet 案例）

"If you don't adapt, your lifetime income drops by over a fifth and your mortality rate in the first year doubles. It actually hurts your health."

David 反驳：avoidance 错误

"The avoidance is the wrong way."

"If we look at any technological shift it's always ended up in net more jobs."
