① ⭐ Self-Improving Skills

"The most basic way to do a self-improvement is after you use the skill and you have a back and forth conversation with the AI because it never gets it right in one shot. Then you just say like hey, based on our conversation, can you please update the skill to account for [the issue] to try to get to it in one shot faster."

完整循环（7 步）

  1. Use skill
  2. Back-and-forth with AI
  3. AI never gets it right in one shot
  4. User: "Based on our conversation, can you please update the skill"
  5. AI makes a bunch of changes
  6. User reviews
  7. Hopefully next time better

Peter 实战频率

"Pretty much every single time I talk to AI, something changes. Like update some skills or something changes."
