Nvidia 的报告框架变更

旧框架

  • Gaming（单独列出）
  • Data Center（compute + networking）

新框架

  • Data Center：Hyperscale（公共云+消费互联网公司）+ ACIE（AI 专用数据中心/工厂）
  • Edge Computing：PC、游戏主机、工作站、AI RAN 基站、机器人、汽车

这意味着什么：

  • Gaming revenue 被并入 edge computing，与机器人/汽车混在一起
  • 无法再单独衡量游戏市场的健康状况
  • Nvidia 故意模糊了游戏业务的下滑

Jensen 的愿景：Personal AI 取代 Personal Computer

"Yesterday's computing was largely about personal computing. In the future, it's going to be about personal AI. The world has a billion users, human users. My sense is that the world is going to have billions of agents. Those billions of agents will all use tools. And those tools are going to be like PCs just like us humans using PCs today. In the future, you'll have an agent using PC." Jensen Huang

AI agents 将有自己的 PC

  • 你给 hyperscaler 钱 → 拥有一个 AI agent → agent 有自己的 computer
  • 你通过 agent 使用计算机
Steve 的反驳：

We are subsidizing the companies with billions of dollars in tax breaks, billions of dollars in property tax breaks while people are paying more in taxes."

  • 游戏 GPU 产量被数据中心挤压
  • RTX 5090 定价过高，普通消费者难以负担

Gamers Nexus 的分析框架

核心论点：这不是转型，是放弃

  1. Nvidia 已经放弃游戏：从"图形公司"→"AI 基础设施公司"→游戏只是 edge computing 的一个子集
  2. 个人计算正在崩溃：数据中心吸走所有资源，内存/存储价格暴涨，PC 出货量两位数下滑
  3. 游戏作为指标：游戏收入下滑 = 家庭计算下滑的信号
  4. Jensen 的原话："NVIDIA is now a data center scale AI infrastructure company reshaping all industries."
"Gaming as a revenue indicator for a company like Nvidia is also an indicator of home computing. It's a marker for it. And if gaming is in decline, then so too is home computing. And that's what we've been saying. But now Nvidia is saying it." Gamers Nexus

Jensen 的"I don't think the United States is a loser"

"I don't think the United States is a loser. You, our industry is now a loser." Jensen Huang
